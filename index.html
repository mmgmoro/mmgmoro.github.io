I FEEL LOVE

Celebrating 40 years of the track that changed everything

In July 1977 Donna Summer, Giorgio Moroder and Pete Bellotte created a hit song that revolutionized dance music and spread a message of love, acceptance and freedom of expression that still resonates today.

In New York, on July 27, Mixmag, Casablanca Records and the Smirnoff Sound Collective will celebrate the anniversary of this amazing record – and its message of love and inclusivity – with its legendary producer, Giorgio Moroder.

Giorgio Moroder and special guests

Thursday, July 27, Schimanski, Brooklyn
Doors at 8pm
